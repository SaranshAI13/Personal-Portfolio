Multi-Asset Risk Intelligence [MARI™]

Python · FastAPI · React.js · Scikit-Learn · SciPy

An enterprise-grade quantitative risk dashboard and model execution engine. It processes historical datasets to solve optimal portfolio allocations and forecast short-horizon volatility using hybrid statistical-ML pipelines.

  • Forecasts 5-day forward realized volatility comparing classical econometric GARCH(1,1) models with a Gradient Boosting Regressor using 14 engineered technical indicators.
  • Solves optimal portfolio allocations under Max Sharpe and Risk Parity constraints, integrating **Ledoit-Wolf Covariance Shrinkage** to denoise covariance estimation.
  • Implements real-time **SHAP local explainability** plots and K-Means clustering to classify market risk regimes.
